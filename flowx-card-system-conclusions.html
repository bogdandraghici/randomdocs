FlowX Card System

The card model for the designer, distilled to two families and a size scale. Primary cards carry a page's structure; Secondary cards group content inside them. Every card shares a 16px radius — the difference is shadow-vs-border and the padding/gap step.

Primary  Cards that structure a page

White surface, drop shadow, no border. Always 24px padding (both axes) and a 16px gap. They sit directly on the grey page background and hold everything else.

Titles and icons are optional. A Primary card may lead with an icon tile + title (the provider card below), carry a title alone (the Evals and “How it tested” cards), or omit both.

Secondary  Cards that group content inside a Primary

neutral-100 1px border, no shadow, 16px radius. They come in three sizes — the only difference is padding and gap.

Titles and icons are optional here too — the Text Generation card pairs a 48px icon tile with a title, the score tiles carry only a label, and the suggestion rows have neither.
